My Interests

I am interested in using X-ray and Optical data to study the compact objects in the cosmos. The AGNs, X-ray binaries, Pulsars, Quasars are some of the compact objects whose complex structure, and emission mechanisms intrigue me. Studying these systems allows us to gain insight into the matter and radiation interaction in extreme conditions and have an impact on a broad range of topics, including accretion flows, pulsar winds, and nuclear physics. Soon, I plan to use my computational and data science skills to study some of these objects and their emission mechanisms with the help of the multi-wavelength data we have in hand.

I am also very fond of deep learning and likes to create and work on my deep learning projects. Apart from these, I also like to do my electronics projects using Arduino and Raspberry pi and play badminton.
